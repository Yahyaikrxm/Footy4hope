3pm - 5pm, July 12th

Tournament Details

We will be raising money for cancer with all proceeds going to this cause.

Register for tournament Cancel Registration

Why

This is a fantastic way to spend quality time with your friends, enjoy the beautiful game, and have a remarkable time supporting a good cause. Along with this, there will be action shot photos, team photos, and a group photo with all participants. At the end of the tournament, we’ll be recognizing both team success and individual excellence with the following awards

    Where?

    On July 12th, from 3pm to 5pm, at Milton Community Field #3. Participants are expected to arrive 15 minutes prior to the start time.

    How

    Logistics: You will receive details via email or phone regarding your team. On the selected date on July 12th, at 3pm you will show up along with your team, prepared to pay the $10 entry fee. Participants are recommended to dress appropriate for the weather conditions, as the event will run rain or shine. It is also suggested to bring soccer cleats, water, a light snack, sun screen, shin pads, and any other necessary gear.

    Game Rules:Matches will be played as scrimmages, starting on a quarter field for the quarterfinals, eventually moving to a half field for both the semifinals and finals. Each game will consist of two 12 minute halfs, with a 2 minute water break at halftime to keep players refreshed and ready.

    The tournament is a 6v6, 8 team tournament
