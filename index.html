Our mission

Kick Cancer out the game

We are turning our passion for soccer into a force for change. By uniting Milton’s youth on the pitch, we aim to raise $10,000 for the Canadian Cancer Society and ensure no one faces cancer alone.

  • Leadership: A tournament designed by students, for students.
  • Total Impact: 100% of all tournament proceeds go directly to the cause.
  • Playing for a Cure: Every goal scored supports life-saving cancer research.

The people behind it

Four friends with one goal

We are a group of dedicated, driven, and passionate high school students from Milton, Ontario looking to end a leading cause of death worldwide, cancer. Each member of our group has been affected by this obstacle, whether it's a family or relative who has faced it.

Tournament Overview

Youth aged 14–18 can compete in a 7-game, 5v5 scrimmage tournament. As teams are eliminated, the playing area expands across the pitch to increase the challenge. Matches are 12 minutes long, featuring a 2-minute halftime to keep players energized.
